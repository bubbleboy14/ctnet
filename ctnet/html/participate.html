CAN Contributor Application
For new or additional abilities to post content on this site, please complete the application below. If/when your application is accepted you will be granted status as a "Provisional CAN Contributor." It works like a political primary - we give you 40 days to keep your status by earning at least 5 positive votes on your content and also maintaining a positive voting percentage above 50% (among people choosing to vote on your content). A positive vote is a 6 or better on a scale of 0-10.
If you succeed in maintaining this level of CAN user approval, you will become an "Active CAN Contributor" after 40 days. Your profile will reflect your success and will remain Active until you are up for election again in one year, at which time you become provisional for another 40 days.
Active contributors can also be impeached if their approval rating falls below 20% for a period of 90 days.
Please note that for fairness reasons and to promote individual representation, Referenda Contributions and Position Papers are not included in this democratic system -- all users are able to post a referendum on the site at anytime.
Please also note that our site will censor any hate speech, unlawful conduct, extreme obscenity or vulgarity, and nudity.
