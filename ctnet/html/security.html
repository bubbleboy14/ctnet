Optional Security Questions
We utilize security questions in the event a user attempts to vote from an unrecognized IP address. For your own protection, please answer at least 3. For more information, click here.
Why Security Questions?
You are here because you haven't answered any security questions. Your responses will help us identify you and keep out unwanted guests.
Is It Safe?
We hash your responses to these questions. This means that we don't know the answers, and there is no way for us or anyone else to retrieve them.
Nevertheless, we can compare your hashed answer with the hash of some later submission. This allows us to confirm your identity without endangering your secrets.
